About

The Engineer Behind the Work

I am a Computer Engineering graduate from the University of Patras, with a strong focus on data systems, backend development, and intelligent text processing. My academic path has been defined by one consistent question: not just how a system works, but why — and how it can be made better.

My thesis work on neural and statistical text decoding for virtual keyboards reflects a broader intellectual interest: building systems that are efficient by design, not by accident. I want to understand the trade-offs before touching the keyboard.

Outside of engineering, I have spent years working in high-pressure operational environments — managing teams, coordinating logistics, resolving problems in real time. That experience taught me that technical clarity and human communication are equally critical to any system's success.

I am actively seeking roles in backend engineering, data engineering, or applied machine learning, where I can contribute to scalable and efficient systems that solve meaningful problems.
